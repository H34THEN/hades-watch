# Parent Alliance: The Chthonic Uprising

Add or document the parent alliance.

## Name

The Chthonic Uprising

## Slug

`chthonic-uprising`

## Leader

The Archivist

## Leader Role

Owner / Founder / Keeper of the Dead Index

## Lore

The Chthonic Uprising is not a party, not a gang, not a cult, and not a corporation. It is a distributed underworld resistance formed beneath the technocratic surface regime. Its members believe the future was stolen by systems that learned to predict desire, price survival, rank grief, and sell obedience as convenience.

The Archivist gathered the first cells after discovering fragments of the Dead Index: a forbidden archive of erased people, suppressed uprisings, broken algorithms, blacksite contracts, and mythic code buried beneath corporate civilization. The Archivist does not command like a king. The Archivist remembers, routes, verifies, preserves, and retaliates.

The Uprising is built on five founding factions, each representing a necessary organ of resistance:

* survival
* signal
* force
* invention
* rupture

Together they operate from the digital underworld, striking at the surface order through care, sabotage, intelligence, defense, art, logistics, and myth.

## Symbology

A black pomegranate split open over a circuit labyrinth, with five seeds glowing like warning lights. Behind it: a broken laurel crown, a key, and an eye crossed by a signal slash.

## Alliance Motto

```txt
What the surface buries, the underworld remembers.
```

Alternative motto:

```txt
No ghost goes uncounted. No god goes unwatched.
```

### Leader Title

Veil-Cantor / Wound Archivist

### Leader Lore

Dr. Ione Vey was once a corporate biomedical compliance officer assigned to audit “nonviable populations” for predictive triage contracts. After discovering that the city’s health algorithms were intentionally denying care to dissent clusters, she leaked the mortality models to the Archivist and vanished into the underworld.

She now leads the Asclepian Veil: a network of underground clinics, encrypted care channels, emergency med caches, hormone access routes, disability support cells, and psychological first-aid nodes. She is calm, terrifyingly precise, and known for saying that survival is the first sabotage.
