# Hades Watch Mission Badge Image Prompts

## Use Notes

Each prompt below is complete and standalone. Copy one full prompt at a time into Nano Banana or your image generator.

All badges are designed to share a uniform website-ready badge format:

- Square 1:1 composition
- Centered symbolic emblem
- Readable at small profile/dossier size
- No character portraits
- No photorealistic objects
- No real-world logos
- No weapons or violent imagery
- No national flags, police insignia, military insignia, or real political symbols
- Consistent Hades Watch myth-tech cyberpunk badge language

The prompts are written so the set remains visually unified while each badge keeps its faction identity.

**Mission:** Black Clinic Readiness  
**Faction Supported:** The Asclepian Veil  
**Badge Text:** STABILIZER  
**Badge Role:** Mission completion badge

